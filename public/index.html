About


This website helps schedule your diet

  • Click on 'Dashboard'
  • Enter Calories, with reference to the Table below
  • Click on 'Get Recommendation'
  • Select entries off of the Tabs viz, Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
  • Click checkout button (blue button)
  • Delete items if they exceed the amount of calories you entered.
  • Select a Date for your Diet Plan, and click Add to write it to the Database
  • Click on 'Calendar' to view all the diet plans you have scheduled.
  • A 'Diet Plan' is hilighted with a yellowish box, on the Calendar
  • Clicking the yellowish-highlight, will let you see the agenda for the day
  • You can delete the Diet Plan for the day after selecting it.

Enter the calories from the table below, which suits your profile. You can access the diet recommendation on the Dashboard-page. These can be selected and then can be viewed in the blue button on Dashboard.

Once the diet scheme is added to the database with a date, it can be viewed on the calendar.
